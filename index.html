Amsterdam, 20 & 21 June 2016

XD Conf is a new ING conference that brings together everyone who believes in the power of design in creating viable and valuable customer experiences, services and (software) products.

We will gather a great international lineup of speakers — technologists, creatives, developers, ops wizards, and community leaders — to provoke and stimulate you with their hard-learned insights on the challenges in designing, delivering and scaling services and products for customers, employees and partners alike.

Join us for two days in June in Amsterdam with like-minded colleagues to teach, learn, and connect with each other and, of course, make new friends. Help us to take our collective skills to the next level and start building the future of ING together.
