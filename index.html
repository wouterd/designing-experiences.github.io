About XD Conf

XD Conf is a new ING conference that brings together everyone involved in designing, building and scaling great customer experiences. This is the place to share, learn and connect with each other, to make new friends and to start building that better future together.

We are gathering a great international lineup of diverse speakers, from API designers and Ops wizards to community leaders and UX innovators. We heartily invite unique and interesting proposals to entertain and educate in any mix of design, technology and business.

Join us for two days in June in Amsterdam to expand your understanding of successful experience design, within and beyond your personal field of expertise.

Location

Amsterdam

The Netherlands' capital city, a short flight from many European cities and overflowing with accomodation choices.

Artis Royal Zoo

The second most visited landmark in Amsterdam, and the greenest location among central Amsterdam canals. Artis is the oldest zoo in the Netherlands and one of the oldest in Europe. Enjoy the diverse plants and animals during Summer Solstice, directly in the heart of Amsterdam.
